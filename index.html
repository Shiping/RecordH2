Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题解答

How to start using hRecord? / 如何开始使用 hRecord？

After installing hRecord, you can directly enter the main interface. Once you allow health data access in the privacy prompt, the app can start tracking your health metrics.

安装 hRecord 后，您可以直接进入主界面。在隐私提示中允许健康数据访问权限后，应用就可以开始追踪您的健康指标了。

What health metrics does hRecord support? / hRecord 支持哪些健康指标？

Currently supports tracking various health metrics including step count, heart rate, sleep quality, and more. You can view all available metric types in the dashboard.

目前支持多种健康指标的追踪，包括步数、心率、睡眠质量等。您可以在仪表盘中查看所有可用的指标类型。

How to add health notes? / 如何添加健康笔记？

Click the "Add Note" button in the app to record any health-related observations or thoughts. These notes will be saved along with your health data.

在应用中点击"添加笔记"按钮，您可以记录任何与健康相关的观察或想法。这些笔记会与您的健康数据一起保存。

How secure is my data? / 数据安全性如何？

All your health data is stored locally on your device. hRecord does not upload any data to the cloud or share with third parties.

您的所有健康数据都存储在本地设备上，hRecord 不会将任何数据上传到云端或与第三方共享。
